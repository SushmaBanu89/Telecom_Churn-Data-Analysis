Key Findings Customer Churn Analysis

Customers with 012 months tenure have the highest churn rate.

Churn rate decreases as tenure increases (more loyal long-term customers).

Churned customers have higher monthly charges but lower total charges (left early).

Senior citizens churn more than younger customers.

Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn; 1-year and 2-year contracts have much lower churn.

Customers paying via electronic check churn more than those using auto-payment methods.

Churn rate is highest in the first 24 months of a customer's lifecycle.
